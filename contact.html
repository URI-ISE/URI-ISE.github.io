Our Lab Spaces

Where the work happens — research environments across the URI Kingston campus.

Cyber-Physical Systems Lab

Tyler Hall, Room 210 · Kingston, RI 02881

Our primary research space for digital twin development, ROS 2 testbeds, and hardware-in-the-loop simulation rigs. Equipped with industrial PLCs, robotic arms, and edge compute clusters.

Manufacturing Intelligence Lab

Wales Hall, Room 105 · Kingston, RI 02881

Dedicated to intelligent scheduling, sensor fusion, and predictive-maintenance research. Houses a small-scale flexible manufacturing cell, vision systems, and GPU workstations for ML model training.

📍 View on Google Maps

Human Factors & Simulation Suite

Fascitelli Center, Room 312 · Kingston, RI 02881

Eye-tracking stations, EEG workload capture, and VR headsets support our human-factors research. Used for operator-performance studies, AR-assisted assembly experiments, and interface usability testing.
