About the Tower of Hanoi

The Tower of Hanoi is a mathematical puzzle consisting of three rods and a number of disks of different sizes which can slide onto any rod.

Rules:

  • Only one disk can be moved at a time
  • Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one stack and placing it on top of another stack
  • No disk may be placed on top of a smaller disk

Optimal Solution: The minimum number of moves required is 2n - 1, where n is the number of disks.
