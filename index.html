Research Areas

Integrated research tracks that connect AI, digital twins, and resilient command systems.

AI & Scheduling

Intelligent Production Optimization

We develop machine-learning models for real-time production scheduling, MES integration, and multi-objective optimization across discrete and continuous manufacturing lines. Our algorithms reduce cycle time and energy consumption while maintaining throughput targets.

  • Deep reinforcement learning for job-shop scheduling
  • Predictive maintenance signal fusion
  • Multi-echelon inventory optimization
Digital Twins

Simulation, Validation & Chaos Engineering

High-fidelity digital twins built on ROS 2 and Unity enable us to validate control logic, inject faults via chaos engineering, and stress-test cyber-physical systems before deployment. We bridge the sim-to-real gap for robotic workcells and logistics hubs.

  • ROS 2 / Gazebo hardware-in-the-loop testbeds
  • Unity-based 3-D visualization dashboards
  • Automated fault-injection and recovery scoring
Resilient C2

Edge-First Command & Control

Our resilient command-and-control frameworks operate in degraded, denied, and intermittent network environments. We combine mobile ad-hoc networking (MANET), zero-knowledge proofs, and edge inference to maintain situational awareness when cloud connectivity is lost.

  • MANET routing under contested RF spectrum
  • Zero-knowledge proof identity verification
  • Federated learning across tactical edge nodes
Supply Chain

End-to-End Supply Network Intelligence

We model, simulate, and optimize global supply networks under disruption. From port congestion to semiconductor shortages, our agent-based and optimization models help decision-makers allocate resources, reroute shipments, and quantify risk in real time.

  • Agent-based disruption propagation modeling
  • Stochastic network flow optimization
  • Blockchain-enabled provenance tracking
Human Factors

Cognitive Load & Operator Performance

We study how operators interact with complex automated systems—measuring cognitive load, decision latency, and trust calibration. Our eye-tracking and physiological sensing experiments inform interface design for manufacturing, defense, and healthcare settings.

  • Eye-tracking & EEG workload assessment
  • Adaptive automation level switching
  • AR/VR-assisted assembly guidance
Cybersecurity

OT/IT Convergence & Threat Modeling

As operational technology (OT) networks converge with enterprise IT, attack surfaces expand. We develop intrusion-detection systems tailored for industrial protocols (Modbus, OPC-UA) and perform red-team exercises on SCADA and PLC environments.

  • ICS anomaly detection via network traffic analysis
  • PLC firmware integrity verification
  • NIST CSF and MITRE ATT&CK for ICS mapping
